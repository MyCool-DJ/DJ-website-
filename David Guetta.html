DAVID GUETTA

Grammy award winner, World-Touring DJ/Producer.

David Guetta

YouTube channel.

My Favourite DJ David Guetta from France made Dance hit masterpieces. When love takes over was one of his first hits to go global in the clubs, this dancy beat was popular in the clubs featuring Kelly Rolland. He won the best remix artist and kept making massive hits, including Titanium feating Sia and play hard with a famous remix of Alice DJ, Better Off Alone as a sample feat Usher. David Guetta is not a singer. He is a producer and a remix artist. He often takes famous parts of songs called a sample where he then remixes the track, changes the key, changes the beat or the tempo, changes the synth to remake the song. A lot of people want to know if a DJ a musician or an artist. This question has plagued the DJ community for decades until a final consensus emerged of Artist. The music was the medium to remix, mash, change key, isolate a frequency (using mixing equipment) and effects, and often paste new lyrics and or a beat behind. This art form was known for its fast-paced beats as the beat of the song should match your heart rate, and when you are dancing, the tempo needs to match the speed of your dancing. Other factors included drinking alcohol. David Guetta made EDM Electronic Dance Music, which means he created his music on a computer. David Guetta is now 53; he did not find success until his late 30's and toured, including winning awards until his 40's. Ironically the music was primarily played in 18+ nightclubs (With the average aged person in their 20's) and produced for those venues in mind. This music was intended to be played loud. David Guetta has never performed in NZ.

RECORD LABEL: Virgin Records (UK) & Capital Records (USA), his own label F#*k I am Famous (FRANCE) and featured on Ministry of sound (but not signed)
